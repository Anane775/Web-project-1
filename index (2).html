About Anex Tours

Anex Tour is an international travel company that was founded in 1996 in Antalya, Turkey. The company is part of the Anex Tourism Group and has become one of the leading tour operators in the tourism industry. Anex Tour offers affordable holiday packages while maintaining high-quality services for travelers around the world.


Anex Tours provides premium travel experiences for tourists around the world. We organize luxury vacations, adventure trips, family holidays, and cultural tours.


Our professional guides and travel experts ensure comfort, safety, and unforgettable memories during every trip.
